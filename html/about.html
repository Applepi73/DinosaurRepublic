About me

Hi!

My name is Malwina Piątkowska. I’m 14 years old and I like dinosaurs. I also like programming in Python and C++. I can play the piano and that’s why I like digits – everything in music is dividable by 2!

One of my multiple talents is translating decimal to binary, octal and hexadecimal code. In my free time, I like to play Binary Fun. It’s a game in which you need to convert decimal to binary, octal or hexadecimal. You are competing with yourself – the faster you convert, the better you are!

Another thing I enjoy is rollerskating, cycling in the forest and diving. In the future, I want to live in the forest so I can get rest from other people. When I was 10 I made my first app – it was very simple. When you clicked the background, its colour changed. I was proud of myself at the time.

How did I get the idea of Dinosaur Republic?

Do you know the Peppa Pig series? And do you know George? Yeah. He inspired me with dinosaurs. I appreciate how he is in love with his dinosaur.

So one day I thought ‘I like making websites – why not make one that has some content?’. It turned out that my knowledge about the graphic side of a website was small. After I discovered CSS, it got easier. You should check out making a website, it's fun!
